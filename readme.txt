## Description

Posts Widget Pro is a powerful and feature-rich WordPress widget designed to display recent posts with advanced customization options. It allows users to showcase their latest content, highlight specific categories, or display posts in a custom order.

## Features

- **Display Recent Posts**: Show your latest blog posts.
- **Advanced Sorting Options**: Sort posts by date, alphabetically, comment count, or randomly.
- **Filter Posts**: Filter posts by categories and tags.
- **Customizable Display**: Choose the number of posts to display.
- **Post Details**: Option to show/hide post date, author, and categories.
- **Thumbnail Support**: Display thumbnails with customizable sizes.
- **Responsive Design**: Works well on all devices.
- **Exclude Current Post**: Option to exclude the current post when viewing a single post.
- **Clean Design**: Minimal and easy-to-use interface.

## Installation

1. Upload the `posts-widget-pro` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to Appearance > Widgets and add the "Posts Widget Pro" widget to your desired widget area.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I display posts from specific categories only?

Yes, you can select one or multiple categories in the widget settings to display posts from those categories only.

### Can I change the order of the displayed posts?

You can sort posts by date, alphabetically, by comment count, or even randomly. You can also choose between ascending and descending order.

### Is it possible to show post thumbnails?

Yes, you can enable post thumbnails in the widget settings. You can also choose the thumbnail size (thumbnail, medium, or large).

### Can I exclude the current post from the widget when viewing a single post?

Yes.
